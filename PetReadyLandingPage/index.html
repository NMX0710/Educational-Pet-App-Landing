PetReady

Play, Learn, Care!

PetReady is an innovative AR app that teaches kids pet care responsibilities. Through interactive tasks, children learn to care for a virtual pet, and parents gain insights into their child's readiness for a real pet.

Why PetReady?

Interactive Tasks: Engaging AR activities for pet care.
Responsibility Score: Assesses readiness for a real pet.
Parental Insights: Detailed reports on child's progress.
Free & Ad-Supported: Accessible to everyone.

Time to get PetReady?

Download & Setup: Create a profile for your child.
Choose a Pet: Select a virtual pet.
Complete Tasks: Daily AR tasks for pet care.
Track Progress: Monitor your child's engagement.
Receive a Score: Get a detailed readiness report.
