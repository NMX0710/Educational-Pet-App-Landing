Discover PetReady

Why PetReady?

Every parent has faced their child's plea for a new pet. Our founders experienced firsthand how quickly the enthusiasm can fade, leaving others to shoulder the responsibility.

PetReady was born from this challenge. We're here to help families understand the commitment of pet ownership before making a significant investment in time and resources.

Our mission is to prepare individuals and families for the joys and challenges of having a pet by providing essential care skills through interactive and engaging lessons.

How PetReady Works

Customized Pet Profiles

Tailor your experience by selecting your pet's breed, age, and special needs. Our AI creates a personalized care plan just for you.

Interactive Learning

Engage with AI-generated content, including summaries, progress reports, and educational videos that bring pet care concepts to life.

Adaptive Curriculum

Experience a dynamic learning journey that evolves with your progress, keeping content challenging and relevant.

Family Dashboard

Track progress across family members and collaborate on pet care tasks with our intuitive dashboard.

Meet Our Team

We're a passionate group of Georgia Tech students, blending expertise in animal care, education, and cutting-edge AI technology. Our diverse experiences drive us to create an impactful solution for pet owners everywhere.

By supporting PetReady, you're not just backing an app – you're championing responsible pet ownership and animal welfare. Together, we can reduce shelter populations and strengthen the bond between humans and their animal companions.

Why We Care

  • As pet lovers and experts, we know that many individuals and families bring home a pet without fully understanding the commitment it requires. This often leads to stress, unpreparedness, and, heartbreakingly, sometimes rehoming. We care deeply about creating a solution that helps people understand what it truly means to care for a pet before they take on the responsibility.
  • But it's more than just teaching responsibility. It's about saving lives. Thousands of pets end up in shelters each year, and many of them never find their forever homes. By educating current and future pet owners, we can reduce the number of pets that end up in shelters, and in turn, reduce the number of animals that face euthanasia in kill shelters.
  • Our goal is to foster empathy, responsibility, and confidence in pet owners of all ages, ensuring they are ready to provide the best care possible for their animal companions. With PetReady, users will learn how to feed, groom, exercise, and care for a pet through AI-tailored interactive lessons, quizzes, and tasks that adapt to any type of pet.
